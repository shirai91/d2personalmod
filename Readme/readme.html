The Forces of Darkness

A Diablo II mod with vanilla LOD gameplay, new graphics and some additional stuff

List of Changes

Quests
  • Act 1:
    • Blood Raven is now located in the new sub-levels in the Graveyard
    • Tree of Inifius is now located in the Stony Field
    • The Moldy Tome is now located in the Dark Woods
    • Horadric Malus and Smith are now located in the Pit Level 2
    • Charsi now always rolls you lvl 99 rare item when imbuing
  • Act 3:
    • Khalim's Eye and Khalim's Brain are now located in the six temples in the Bazaar, Upper Kurast, and the Causeway
    • Ormus now gives you rare amulet instead of rare ring
  • Act 5:
    • Larzuk now gives you maximum number of sockets for any item quality
    • Qual-Kehk now gives you Unidentified Rune instead of Ral Ort Tal
    • Baal now gives you a new quest reward in Hell difficulty
  • World Event:
    • Token of Absolution replaced with Diablo's Soulstone (sell it to let Diablo walk the Earth)

Items
  • Socketable belts, boots and gloves
  • Arrows, bolts, throwing weapons, scrolls and keys no longer consume quantity
  • Rare charms can be found
  • Magic items now always spawn with affix and prefix
  • Rare items now always spawn with maximum number of prefixes
  • Unique and set items drop already identified
  • Some items now display additional information

Cube Recipes

Potions:
3 Health Potions of the Same Type (lower than Super) = 1 Higher Grade Health Potion
3 Mana Potions of the Same Type (lower than Super) = 1 Higher Grade Mana Potion
3 Rejuvenation Potions = 1 Full Rejuvenation Potion

Runes:
3 Runes of the Same Type (El - Lem) = 1 Higher Level Rune
2 Runes of the Same Type (Pul - Cham) = 1 Higher Level Rune
Any Rune (Higher than El) + 2 Jewels = 1 Lower Level Rune

Gems:
3 Gems of the Same Type and Grade (lower than Perfect) = 1 Higher Grade Gem of the Same Type

Jewelry:
3 Magic Rings = Rare Ring
3 Magic Amulets = Rare Amulet
3 Magic Jewels = Rare Jewel

Sockets:
1 Tal Rune + 1 Thul Rune + 1 Perfect Topaz + Normal Body Armor = Socketed Body Armor of same type
1 Ral Rune + 1 Amn Rune + 1 Perfect Amethyst + Normal Weapon = Socketed Weapon of same type
1 Ral Rune + 1 Thul Rune + 1 Perfect Sapphire + Normal Helm = Socketed Helm of same type
1 Tal Rune + 1 Amn Rune + 1 Perfect Ruby + Normal Shield = Socketed Shield of same type
1 Ith Rune + 1 Ort Rune + 1 Perfect Amber + Normal Gloves = Socketed Gloves of same type
1 Eth Rune + 1 Ral Rune + 1 Perfect Obsidian + Normal Boots = Socketed Boots of same type
1 Nef Rune + 1 Tal Rune + 1 Perfect Aquamarine + Normal Belt = Socketed Belt of same type

Upgrade:
1 Ort Rune + 1 Amn Rune + 1 Perfect Sapphire + Normal Rare Weapon = Exceptional Rare Weapon
1 Ral Rune + 1 Thul Rune + 1 Perfect Amethyst + Normal Rare Armor = Exceptional Rare Armor
1 Fal Rune + 1 Um Rune + 1 Perfect Sapphire + Exceptional Rare Weapon = Elite Rare Weapon
1 Ko Rune + 1 Pul Rune + 1 Perfect Amethyst + Exceptional Rare Armor = Elite Rare Armor
1 Ral Rune + 1 Sol Rune + 1 Perfect Emerald + Normal Unique Weapon = Exceptional Version of Weapon
1 Tal Rune + 1 Shael Rune + 1 Perfect Diamond + Normal Unique Armor = Exceptional Version of Armor
1 Lum Rune + 1 Pul Rune + 1 Perfect Emerald + Exceptional Unique Weapon = Elite Version of Weapon
1 Ko Rune + 1 Lem Rune + 1 Perfect Diamond + Exceptional Unique Armor = Elite Version of Armor

Unsocket:
